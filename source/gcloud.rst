Gcloud
======

Contains drivers that interactive with gcloud assets

Installation
------------

Include the following section in your **init.yaml** under **repos** section::

  - repo: https://github.com/honeydipper/honeydipper-config-essentials
    branch: DipperCL
    path: /gcloud
